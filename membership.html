Membership Application

Why Join YSP?

  • Participate in meaningful community service projects
  • Develop leadership and professional skills
  • Connect with like-minded youth from across the country
  • Access training and development opportunities
  • Make a lasting impact in your community

Requirements:

  • Must be between 18-30 years old
  • Filipino citizen or resident
  • Commitment to community service
  • Willingness to participate in YSP activities
Apply for Membership

Start a Chapter

Why Start a Chapter?

  • Lead a team of passionate young volunteers in your area
  • Build community connections and create lasting networks
  • Implement YSP programs tailored to your local needs
  • Gain organizational and leadership experience
  • Expand YSP's impact across more regions

Chapter Requirements:

  • Must be an existing YSP member for at least 3 months
  • Demonstrate strong commitment and leadership abilities
  • Have a minimum of 5 interested volunteers in your area
  • Be willing to organize and lead regular activities
